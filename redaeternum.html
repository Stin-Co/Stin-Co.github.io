What is Red Aeternum?


Red Aeternum is an open world RPG set in a dieselpunk universe scarred by its past.


You play as J-0801, a genetically modified human woken up thousands of years after the war they were created to fight. You must now choose a side--or none at all--in the middle of a new conflict that will play out under your influence. Operate ground vehicles, spacecraft, and heavy weapons as you scavenge for resources to survive on a ruined planet of your own creation.


Red Aeternum is an extremely customizable experience, where players can dial in each element of the game to their liking. Mechanically accurate guns that can be disassembled and upgraded, modifiable vehicles & bases, and programmable robots are just a few of the systems that will appear.


The game is a two-person project that is currently in development, and we plan to have a playable demo available in 2026.
